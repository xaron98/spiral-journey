What is Spiral Journey?

An iOS and watchOS app that visualizes your complete 24-hour cycle — sleep and wakefulness — on a spiral. Not a simple sleep tracker: a chronobiological instrument based on circadian science.

Built on a new sleep model. Traditional apps divide sleep into 5 artificial stages (N1, N2, N3, REM, Wake). Research with 155+ subjects across 2 independent datasets reveals that sleep naturally has 3 states: an active pole (Wake + REM), a continuous depth gradient (NREM), and REM as a distinct consciousness state geometrically close to wakefulness.

Key findings (based on ongoing research)

  • 3 natural states, not 5: Wake, NREM (continuous depth gradient), and REM — validated across 142 subjects (HMC dataset) and 13 recordings (Sleep-EDF).
  • Two geometric poles: Active pole (Wake + REM, less than 3.5 degrees apart) and Deep pole (NREM continuum, 12-14 degrees apart). The structure is universal across electrode locations.
  • NREM is continuous: Sleep depth is a gradient measured by the winding number, not discrete N1/N2/N3 categories.
  • REM is not between NREM and Wake: Geometrically, REM is almost identical to Wake — it belongs to the active pole, with muscles disconnected.

3D Double Helix 3D

Interactive RealityKit visualization comparing two time periods as a molecular DNA model.

  • Gold backbone: Current period (today / this week)
  • Silver backbone: Comparison period (yesterday / last week / your best)
  • Colored bars: Sleep phases in each 30-minute interval — amber (wake), violet (REM), blue gradient (NREM light to deep)
  • Tap any bar: See the exact time and phase for both nights
  • Drag to rotate, pinch to zoom
