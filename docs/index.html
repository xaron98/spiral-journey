Spiral Journey

Sleep · Wake Cycle Tracker

About

Spiral Journey is a circadian rhythm tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch. It uses Cosinor Analysis, Phase Response Curves, and the Two-Process Model to help you understand and improve your sleep patterns.

Features

  • Circadian spiral visualization of your sleep/wake cycle
  • Cosinor analysis — acrophase, MESOR, amplitude, R²
  • Phase Response Curve (PRC) for light, melatonin, and exercise
  • Social jet lag detection
  • Rephase mode — gradually shift your circadian schedule
  • Apple Health integration
  • Apple Watch companion app with complications
  • 8 languages: English, Español, Català, Deutsch, Français, 中文, 日本語, العربية
