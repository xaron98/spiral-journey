Spiral Journey

Circadian Rhythm & Sleep DNA Tracker

About

Spiral Journey is a circadian rhythm tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch. It goes beyond simple sleep tracking — it models your sleep as biological DNA, detecting invisible patterns, predicting your sleep using sequence alignment, and reflecting your circadian health through a narrative "biological mirror."

Core Features

  • Circadian spiral visualization of your sleep/wake cycle (2D Canvas)
  • Cosinor analysis — acrophase, MESOR, amplitude, R²
  • Phase Response Curve (PRC) for light, melatonin, and exercise
  • Social jet lag detection
  • Rephase mode — gradually shift your circadian schedule
  • Apple Health integration
  • Apple Watch companion app with complications
  • 8 languages: English, Español, Català, Deutsch, Français, 中文, 日本語, العربية

Sleep DNA Engine New

Your sleep patterns are encoded as a double helix — like biological DNA. Two strands (sleep data + life context) intertwine, revealing patterns invisible to traditional analysis.

🧬

Nucleotide Encoding

Each day becomes a 16-feature vector — bedtime, wake, duration, Process S, circadian rhythm, caffeine, exercise, stress, and more. Days form weekly "sequences" for comparison.

🔬

DTW Sequence Alignment

Dynamic Time Warping finds weeks that resemble your current one — "your week looks like February 15th (87% similar)." Predicts what comes next based on historical patterns.

🧪

Motif Discovery

Discovers your recurring "sleep genes" — patterns like "Monday-crash" or "Recovery-weekend." Classifies deviations as silent, missense, or nonsense mutations.

⚡

Adaptive SleepBLOSUM

A personalized scoring matrix that learns which factors matter most for YOUR sleep. Uses mutual information to weight features — making pattern detection unique to each user.

Health Markers New

  • HB — Homeo-Circadian Balance: Measures the "fight" between your circadian clock and sleep pressure
  • RDS — REM Drift Slope: Tracks how your REM phases shift across the night
  • HCI — Helical Continuity Index: Geometric measure of sleep fragmentation
  • RCE — REM Cluster Entropy: Coherence of your REM episode distribution
  • Circadian Coherence: Overall rhythm stability (anarchy detection)
  • Phase Drift Severity: How fast your bedtime is shifting

Hilbert Phase Synchrony New

Uses the Hilbert Transform (via Accelerate FFT) to compute Phase Locking Values between sleep features and life context — revealing which factors truly affect your sleep and with what lag. "Caffeine affects your bedtime with a 1-day delay."

AI Coach AI

  • Dual-path architecture: Foundation Models (iOS 26+) with Phi-3.5 GGUF fallback
  • 100% on-device: All inference runs locally — no data leaves your phone
  • DNA-aware: Coach receives motif patterns, health markers, and alignment data for contextual responses
  • Provider-aware prompts: Rich tier (Foundation Models) includes tonight's prediction and model accuracy

ML Prediction Pipeline AI

  • Three engines: Neural Network (CoreML), Gradient Boosting, and Sequence Alignment
  • On-device retraining: MLUpdateTask with 80/20 validation split and regression guard
  • Continuous Process S: Sleep pressure tracked across days with debt memory
  • Personalized models: Fine-tuned to your data after 60 evaluated predictions

3D Double Helix 3D

Interactive RealityKit visualization of your sleep DNA as a vertical double helix. Rotate, zoom, tap weeks to highlight similar patterns, and toggle motif regions — all inline in the DNA Insights view.

  • Purple strand: Sleep data (bedtime, wake, duration, rhythm)
  • Orange strand: Life context (caffeine, exercise, stress, weekends)
  • Quality-colored nucleotides: Green (good) → Red (poor) per day
  • Geometric encoding: Twist = phase synchrony, Radius = chronotype deviation, Thickness = deep sleep, Roughness = fragmentation

DNA Insights — Biological Mirror New

Not a dashboard — a narrative reflection of your circadian biology. Opens from the 🧬 icon in the spiral view.

  • "Tu ritmo hoy" — Synchronized / In transition / Misaligned (color-coded)
  • "Tu código genético" — Active motif pattern + mutations
  • "Déjà vu" — Similar weeks from your history + alignment prediction
  • "Tu salud circadiana" — Health alerts (only when relevant)
  • "Qué afecta tu sueño" — Top base pairs (PLV-ranked)
  • Three-tier degradation: basic (<4 weeks) → intermediate → full (8+ weeks)

Data Architecture

  • SwiftData: Local-only persistence (CKSyncEngine handles CloudKit separately)
  • Migration: Automatic one-shot JSON → SwiftData on first launch
  • Background task: Daily DNA profile computation at 4 AM
  • Watch sync: Observer-based bridge to App Group UserDefaults
  • Data retention: Chat (100 messages), metrics (90 days), predictions (kept forever for ML)

Privacy

All processing happens on your device. No cloud AI, no data collection, no tracking. Your sleep DNA stays with you.
