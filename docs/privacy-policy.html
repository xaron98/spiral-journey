Spiral Journey

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Spiral Journey ("the app") is a circadian rhythm tracker for iOS and Apple Watch. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

Apple Health / HealthKit

With your explicit permission, the app reads sleep analysis data from Apple Health to:

  • Calculate your circadian rhythm metrics (acrophase, MESOR, amplitude)
  • Detect sleep patterns and irregularities
  • Provide personalized recommendations

The app does not write any data to Apple Health.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, location)
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking
  • No analytics services or third-party SDKs
  • No advertising or analytics data is transmitted to third-party tracking servers

Data Storage

Primary app data (sleep episodes, events, settings) is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled on your Apple account, app records may be synced using Apple CloudKit private database so your own devices stay in sync.

On-Device AI Model Download

If you enable the AI coach chat feature, the app downloads an open GGUF model file from Hugging Face to your device. Prompts and generated responses are processed on-device after download.

Apple Watch

Data synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication is direct device-to-device and is never routed through external servers.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
